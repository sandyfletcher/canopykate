A Fresh Brand Identity for a Local Cafe

From logo design to a complete visual system that tells a story of community and craft.

Client

The Daily Grind

Services

Brand Strategy
Logo Design
Packaging

Year

2023

The Challenge

The Daily Grind, a beloved local cafe, needed to refresh its visual identity to stand out in a growing market. The goal was to create a brand that felt warm, modern, and deeply connected to the local community, while also being flexible enough for new packaging and digital menus.

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive brand identity centered around a custom logotype and a warm, earthy color palette. The system included new menu designs, coffee cup packaging, and a set of versatile patterns inspired by coffee beans and local flora. The result is a cohesive and inviting brand that reflects the heart of the cafe and resonates with its customers.
